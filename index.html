⚠️ This is a hobby project. It is not truly private or secure. Messages and metadata live in Firebase and may be visible to the project owner. Don’t share personal info.

How it works

You join a queue. When two people are waiting, a room is created. When a user leaves, the room closes. You can requeue to meet someone new. This runs entirely on GitHub Pages + Firebase.
